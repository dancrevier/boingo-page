WiFi Scale Tracker is a Windows Phone application for tracking your weight as measured by a Withings WiFi Body Scale, written by Dan Crevier. This application is not usable without such a scale and requires you to use your my.withings.com account. It supports the following features:

  • Metro style UI
  • Shows most recent weight in pounds
  • Shows daily, weekly and monthly trends based on simple linear regression
  • Can show all weight measurements
  • Supports pounds and kilograms
  • Displays BMI
  • High and low weights
  • Share weight on social networks
  • Displays graph of weight over time
    • Pinch zoom to change range
    • Drag to pan the graph
    • Double tap to zoom out
    • Tap and hold to see measurement values
