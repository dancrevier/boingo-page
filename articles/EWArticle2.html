Review of Music for a Darkened Theatre

by Ty Burr Entertainment Weekly, 1992

Music for a Darkened Theatre--Danny Elfman (MCA) The erstwhile leader of the rock group Oingo Boingo has become the bad boy of movie music--there's no orchestral effect he won't try, and the results are frisky and ingenious. This compilaton of his scores covers everything from the riotous ("Pee-wee's Big Adventure") to the murkily dazzling ("Batman").
