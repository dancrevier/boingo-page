Boingo Review

By Josef Woodard
Entertainment Weekly, 5/20/94

Movie-score composer and Oingo Boingo leader Danny Elfman tries to make peace with his two claims to fame. The first new album from Boingo (ixnay the Oingo) in four years is full of frothy charm, stalwart pop craft, and some shameless Beatlesesquirie (including a cover of "I Am the Walrus"). On the down side, the sproinging dance ethic and overblown rock theatrics taste a bit stale, and the orchestral maneuvers amount to extraneous padding. B-
