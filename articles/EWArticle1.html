Music: Danny Elfman

Entertainment Weekly, 1992

MUSIC:Danny Elfman, front man of weirdo rock group Oingo Boingo and composer of "Batman", "Dick Tracy", and "The Simpsons", is embarking on a new career, this time as a screenwriter. Elfman is developing several oddball projects, including an "over-the-top" musical titled "The World of Jimmy Callicut" at Fox and "a strange and stylized ghost story" he'll also direct called "Julian", which Tim Burton is executive producing for Warner Bros...
