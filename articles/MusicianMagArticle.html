Musician Magazine

1992

DANNY ELFMAN
MUSIC THAT CHANGED MY LIFE

"Stravinsky's The Rite of Spring. A friend who was a trumpet player played it for me when I was 16 or 17 in high school. I've no idea what recording it was. Within a year I'd thrown out my enire collection of rock albums, so I guess you could say it had a big effect. It was a whole new world--it was like discovering a whole new planet, one I'd never known"
