INSANITY (Farewell version)

From DAVID SALLAY (SALLAYD@m.binghs.jordan.k12.ut.us)

During the verse of this song it is just screwing around on C#m.  The 
bass just plays a pedal on a 6-string bass on C# (I wouldn't know that 
without the farewell video)  The only part with actual chords is the 
chorus's, they are the following:
	E	A	D	G	B	e
C#m: 	9	11	11	9	x	x
C#dim:	9	10	8	8	x	x
G/(C#)*	9	10	12	(12)	x	x
F#/C#	9	9	11	(11)	x	x
A/E	12	12	14	(14)	x	x
D#	x	18	20	20	x	x
(*the C# is really there with the orchestra but sometimes it doesn't 
sound right on the guitar)

this fill is also used sometimes - I'll call it fill 1
e--------------------------|
B--------------------------|
G--21--20------------------|
D----------19--------------|
A--------------------------|
E--------------------------| 
at the time I do not have a guitar on me so this maybe a little off, 
listen to the recording and you should know it is and figure it out 
yourself if I indeed am incorrect.
